Our Services

Antenatal

Our comprehensive antenatal care services provide expectant mothers with the support and medical attention they need throughout their pregnancy.

Postnatal

After giving birth, our postnatal care services focus on the well-being of both the mother and the newborn.

Impatient Care

Our inpatient care services are designed to provide a comfortable and healing environment for patients who need overnight stays or extended medical treatment.

Family planning

Our dedicated family planning services offer a range of contraceptive options, counseling, and guidance to help you choose the most suitable method for your lifestyle and future plans.

Xray

Our state-of-the-art X-ray facility is equipped with advanced imaging technology to assist in diagnosing and evaluating various medical conditions.

Ultra Scanning

Our ultrasound scanning services utilize advanced equipment to provide detailed and real-time imaging of internal organs and structures.

Labour & Delivery

Our experienced team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to ensuring a safe and comfortable childbirth experience for both mother and baby.

Surgical Operation

In cases where surgical intervention is necessary, our skilled surgeons perform a wide range of procedures to address various medical conditions.
